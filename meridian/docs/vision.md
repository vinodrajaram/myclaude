# MERIDIAN — Platform Vision

## Vision Statement

**To become the operating system for the modern banker** — the single intelligent platform where every piece of client intelligence, deal activity, market signal, credit insight, and institutional knowledge converges, enabling bankers to act faster, smarter, and with greater confidence than was ever possible before.

---

## Mission

Remove the friction between intelligence and action.

Bankers today spend the majority of their time in unproductive work: hunting for information across disconnected systems, manually preparing materials that could be auto-drafted, tracking deal progress in spreadsheets, and piecing together credit pictures from 12 different sources. MERIDIAN eliminates this — replacing fragmentation with synthesis, repetition with automation, and reactive work with proactive intelligence.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Today's banking technology stack is a patchwork:
- **CRM** stores contacts but provides no intelligence
- **Data terminals** surface market data but require manual synthesis
- **Document tools** require starting from scratch every time
- **Credit systems** are disconnected from deal origination
- **Email and chat** are the de facto workflow tools — completely unstructured

The result: senior bankers spend 60%+ of their time on work that adds no value to clients. Junior bankers are buried in manual preparation. Deals are lost because no one had the full picture at the right moment.

---

## AI-First Philosophy

MERIDIAN is not a CRM with an AI chatbot bolted on. It is AI-native at every layer:

1. **Intelligence over information** — Data is only useful when synthesized. MERIDIAN surfaces insights, not raw data.
2. **Proactive, not reactive** — The platform surfaces what matters before you ask for it: a client's earnings miss, a trigger event, a covenant breach approaching.
3. **Draft, don't start** — Any content that can be auto-drafted, is. Pitch books, credit memos, briefing notes — generated in seconds, refined by humans.
4. **Context everywhere** — Every module knows everything else. The deal pipeline knows the credit profile. The content factory knows the meeting history.
5. **Minimal interface** — The AI handles complexity. The interface stays clean. Bankers see what they need, not everything that exists.

---

## Design Principles

### 1. Persona-Driven
Every module, every screen, every default is calibrated to a specific role. An MD sees strategic relationship health and pipeline value. An Analyst sees preparation checklists and data pulls. A Credit Officer sees covenant dashboards and risk flags. Same platform, tuned experiences.

### 2. Connected
All modules share a common data fabric. Actions in one module immediately inform others. Adding a new contact to the CRM auto-enriches the deal pipeline. Booking a client meeting triggers briefing note generation. Covenant monitoring flags feed directly into relationship risk scoring.

### 3. Real-Time
MERIDIAN operates on live data wherever possible. Market signals update continuously. News is synthesized as it breaks. Client activity is logged in real-time. The platform is always current.

### 4. Secure and Compliant
Financial services have the most demanding regulatory environment in any industry. MERIDIAN is built with compliance as a first principle: Chinese walls enforced at the data layer, full audit trails, MNPI controls, GDPR/data residency, and role-based access that mirrors institutional org structures.

### 5. Elegant and Fast
Speed is a product feature. Every interaction must feel instant. Every screen must be clean. Complexity lives in the AI layer, not the interface. We are not building another dense enterprise tool — we are building the tool bankers actually want to use.

## North Star Metric

**Time-to-insight** — the elapsed time between a banker identifying a question and having a confident, actionable answer. MERIDIAN's mission is to drive this toward zero.

Secondary metrics:
- Deals originated per banker per quarter
- Pitch books produced per analyst per week
- Covenant breaches caught before default
- Client meeting satisfaction scores

A world where:

- Every pitch book is 80% drafted before anyone opens a deck

- Every deal opportunity is tracked from first signal to mandate close
